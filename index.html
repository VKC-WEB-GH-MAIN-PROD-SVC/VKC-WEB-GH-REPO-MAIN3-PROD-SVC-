Technical Design Authority Advisory

Archonex is a boutique enterprise Microsoft architecture advisory practice providing defensible-by-design technical design authority for Microsoft 365 and Azure environments.

Engagements are structured around governance checkpoints, control catalogues and evidence-ready artefacts designed to withstand scrutiny from security leadership, governance boards and auditors.

Archonex is best suited to complex, multi-team environments — typically organisations operating Microsoft estates of 1,000+ users — and delivers formal design-authority-ready outputs including HLD/LLD packs, control catalogues and architecture decision artefacts.

Architecture governance framework

Every engagement is delivered with formal artefacts and governance checkpoints. The output is designed to be defensible: clear ownership, clear decisions, clear evidence.

What we deliver

  • Control Catalogue mapped to identity, endpoint, collaboration and data domains
  • HLD / LLD pack with NFRs, guardrails, SLAs and operating assumptions
  • Design Authority papers (options appraisal, risks, recommendation, decision)
  • Runbooks including cutover/rollback and validation steps
  • RACI ownership and BAU acceptance criteria

How decisions remain defensible

  • Scope boundaries documented with explicit exclusions
  • Exceptions time-boxed with risk acceptance and review cadence
  • Controls mapped from objective → enforcement → evidence
  • Operational telemetry aligned to governance checkpoints
  • Executive summaries suitable for board review
