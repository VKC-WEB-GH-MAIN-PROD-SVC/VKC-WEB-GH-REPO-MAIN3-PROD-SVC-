Governance Framework

Every engagement is delivered as a structured governance package designed to be defensible under audit and board scrutiny.

  • HLD/LLD + NFRs + SLAs + operating assumptions
  • Control catalogue mapped to enforcement and evidence

  • Executive summaries suitable for board review
